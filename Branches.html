African Village

Kuriftu African Village is a unique resort located in Burayu, Ethiopia, that offers guests an immersive experience into the diverse cultures and traditions of Africa. The village features traditional African-style accommodations, including huts and cottages, designed to reflect the architectural styles of various African regions. Guests can enjoy cultural performances, traditional cuisine, and various recreational activities such as horseback riding and swimming. Kuriftu African Village aims to provide an authentic and enriching experience that celebrates the rich heritage of the African continent.
